Laissez les bons temps rouler!

Let the good times roll!


My neighborhood is family oriented but has plenty of great restaurants a block away. It has this really great doughnut shop within walking distance from my house. It is so good that it's comparable to Krispy Kreme. YUM!

The Broussard Sports Complex is only a few miles down the road. There you can practice soccer, baseball/softball, and basketball. There are running trails, fishing ponds, and playgrounds for the little ones as well. There's even a splash pad! Don't forget to bring extra clothes!!
